About

My name is Margaret Salvani. I am a student at Freehold High School. I’m a hardworking, diligent, and creative student. I’m currently in the Honors Web Design class in the Web Design Pathway. My interests are graphic design, and video game development. My current skillset is Python, HTML, CSS, Photoshop, Javascript, Bootstrap, and C++.

My career goal is to create video games. Although I haven't made up my mind, I would like to work as an independent developer or for a major corporation like Sony or EA. Work location is not a concern for me because I wish to travel. Though I personally enjoy coding more, I would love to be in charge of the game's design and code.

My aspiration is influenced by independent game developers and YouTubers who shared their game creation process. I adored watching them solve glitches and problems, design breathtaking settings, and develop likable, believable characters. They exuded a constant passion for their games and their storytelling abilities. The success of indie developers in creating their games inspired me to pick up programming so that I could eventually use video games to tell my own imaginative stories.
